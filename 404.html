NADRA Introduction: The National Database and Registration Authority is an independent body of the Ministry of Interior of Pakistan, which is responsible for maintaining data on all citizen data. It was incorporated as the National Database Organization in 1998 and merged with NADRA in 2000. The National Database and Registration Authority has received international recognition for its latest and unique principles. The government has invested time and money in simplifying and modernizing its technology to go online with e-governance mode. NADRA is an independent sector governed by the policy formulated by the government. Offers a highly successful career with competitive salary plans in the market. Employees here are selected with great care and responsibility, because the office contains sensitive information of all citizens. Available positions: Assistant to the Works Council/Assistant to the Director Works Council/Deputy Director Senior Corporate Council / Director The position of the National Database and Registration Office is open for the Senior Director/Director of the Enterprise Committee, the Enterprise Committee/Deputy Director and the Deputy Enterprise Committee/Assistant Director. These job opportunities are opening in Islamabad. Professionals who have baccalaureate/masters degrees in law can apply for these job opportunities. These positions require a minimum of 5 to 6 months of experience. Maximum age limit is 25 years.
